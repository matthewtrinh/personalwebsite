About Me


An economics and data science trained consultant turned software engineer.

If you are on this website, I'm assuming you are either a hiring manager or some weirdo that found this website by chance. I really hope it's the former.

Either way, it's nice to meet you! My name is Matthew Trinh. I double majored in Economics and ACMS (Applied and Computational Mathematics and Statistics) and minored in Data Science at the University of Notre Dame. After graduating, I went into consulting because I enjoy solving unique problems and being challenged to use my brain in new and different ways.

Having programmed throughout high school and college, I quickly found myself missing the excitement I feel when I code and yearned to code more. So not wasting any time, I left my consulting job and dove head-first into learning as much as I could and strengthening my programming skills. I bolstered my back-end programming skills in Python, R, and SQL and heavily dove into the world of front-end programming with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I'm still enthusiastically learning and started studying ReactJS, NodeJS, MongoDB, and ExpressJS. With the skills I have gained from my experiences at Notre Dame, internships, consulting, and continuing studies, I bring a unique perspective, a love for learning, frightening levels of perseverance and resilience akin to the 0.01% of germs that survive hand sanitizer, and will become a great asset to your team.
